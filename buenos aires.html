Top Spots in Buenos Aires

Discover the vibrant culture, stunning architecture, and rich history of Argentina's capital.

Obelisco
An iconic landmark in the heart of Buenos Aires, commemorating the 400th anniversary of the city's founding.
Teatro Colón
A world-renowned opera house with stunning acoustics and grand architecture.
Plaza de Mayo
A historic square at the heart of Buenos Aires, home to the Casa Rosada and the country's political history.
La Boca
A colorful neighborhood known for its vibrant murals, tango culture, and the famous Caminito street.
Jardín Botánico
A tranquil botanical garden with lush greenery, perfect for a relaxing stroll or picnic.
