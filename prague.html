Top Spots in Prague

Explore the stunning architecture, rich history, and vibrant culture of the Czech capital.

Charles Bridge
A historic stone bridge connecting Prague Castle with the Old Town, offering beautiful views of the Vltava River.
Prague Castle
The largest ancient castle in the world, offering stunning views of the city and home to the Czech kings.
Astronomical Clock
Located in the Old Town Hall, this medieval clock is famous for its intricate design and hourly show.
Old Town Square
A historic square in the heart of Prague, home to medieval buildings and the famous Astronomical Clock.
Petřín Hill
A green space with gardens and a lookout tower that resembles Paris's Eiffel Tower, offering panoramic views of the city.
