Top Spots in Amsterdam

Explore the charming canals, world-class museums, and vibrant neighborhoods of Amsterdam.

Anne Frank House
The historic house where Anne Frank hid during World War II, now a museum dedicated to her life and legacy.
Rijksmuseum
One of Amsterdam's most famous museums, housing works by Rembrandt, Vermeer, and other Dutch masters.
Van Gogh Museum
A museum dedicated to the works of Vincent van Gogh, featuring some of his most iconic paintings.
Vondelpark
The largest park in Amsterdam, a green oasis perfect for picnics, cycling, and relaxing walks.
Canal Cruise
A quintessential Amsterdam experience, offering a scenic tour through the city's iconic canal network.
