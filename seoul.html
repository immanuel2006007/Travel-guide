Top Spots in Seoul

Explore the blend of ancient history and modern innovation in South Korea's vibrant capital.

Gyeongbokgung Palace
The grandest of Seoul's five royal palaces, offering a glimpse into Korea's past dynasties.
Namsan Seoul Tower
A landmark tower offering panoramic views of the city, especially beautiful at night.
Bukchon Hanok Village
A traditional village with well-preserved hanok houses, offering a glimpse of old Seoul amidst the modern city.
Myeongdong Shopping Street
One of Seoul's busiest shopping districts, known for its fashion, beauty stores, and street food stalls.
Hangang Park
A scenic riverside park perfect for biking, picnics, or enjoying a peaceful sunset along the Han River.
