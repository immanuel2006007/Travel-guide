Top Spots in Marrakech

Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture, rich history, and stunning architecture of this ancient city.

Jemaa el-Fnaa
The bustling central square in Marrakech, filled with street performers, food stalls, and vibrant markets.
Koutoubia Mosque
The largest mosque in Marrakech, known for its stunning minaret visible from many parts of the city.
Bahia Palace
An exquisite palace with beautiful gardens and intricate Moroccan architecture.
Majorelle Garden
A stunning garden designed by French painter Jacques Majorelle, home to a variety of plants and vibrant blue buildings.
El Badi Palace
A historic palace, now in ruins, known for its vast courtyards and intricate design.
