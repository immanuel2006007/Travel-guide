Top Spots in London

Explore the rich history, iconic landmarks, and vibrant culture of London's heart.

Big Ben
One of London's most iconic landmarks, the clock tower of the Palace of Westminster.
London Eye
A giant Ferris wheel offering panoramic views of the city along the River Thames.
Tower Bridge
A Victorian bascule bridge that spans the River Thames, known for its distinctive twin towers.
Buckingham Palace
The official residence of the British monarch, famous for the Changing of the Guard ceremony.
British Museum
One of the world's greatest museums, housing a vast collection of art and artifacts from ancient history.
