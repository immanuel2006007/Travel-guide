Top Spots in Bangkok

Experience the vibrant culture, grand temples, and dynamic street life in the heart of Thailand's capital.

Grand Palace
A stunning complex of buildings that served as the official residence of the kings of Siam.
Wat Arun
A beautiful temple known for its iconic spires, located on the banks of the Chao Phraya River.
Chatuchak Market
One of the largest markets in the world, offering everything from clothing to food and antiques.
Damnoen Saduak Floating Market
A traditional floating market where vendors sell fresh produce, souvenirs, and food from boats.
Lumphini Park
A peaceful park in the middle of the city, perfect for relaxing, jogging, or paddle boating.
