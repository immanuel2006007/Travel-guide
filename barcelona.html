Top Spots in Barcelona

Explore the architectural wonders, vibrant culture, and beautiful beaches of Barcelona.

Sagrada Familia
One of Antoni Gaudí's most iconic works, this basilica is still under construction after more than a century.
Park Güell
A public park designed by Antoni Gaudí, famous for its colorful mosaics and unique architecture.
La Rambla
A bustling street lined with shops, cafes, and street performers, perfect for a stroll through the city.
Barceloneta Beach
A popular beach where locals and tourists alike enjoy the Mediterranean sun and sea.
Montjuïc Hill
A hill offering panoramic views of the city, with attractions like the Magic Fountain and the Olympic Stadium.
